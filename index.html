Yahtzee!

How to Play Yahtzee!

  1. Roll the Dice: At the start of your turn, roll all five dice.
  2. Select Dice to Hold: After the first roll, choose which dice to keep (hold) and which to re-roll based on your target scoring category.
  3. Re-Roll (Up to 2 More Times): You may roll up to two additional times, adjusting which dice you hold to improve your combination.
  4. Choose a Scoring Category: At the end of your turn, pick an available category on your scorecard. Assign your dice result to that category—the category can’t be used again.
  5. Record Your Score: Calculate the score for that category using the corresponding rules (such as summing dice for upper-section numbers or specific patterns like full house).
  6. Repeat Turns: Continue taking turns until every category on the scorecard has been filled.
  7. Determine the Winner: Tally all the scores; the player with the highest total wins the game.
